$$P_{\hat B}(b) - \bar P_{\hat B|\hat A}(b) = \int \text{d}a\,\operatorname{tr}([\hat A_a \hat B_b, \hat B_b \hat A_a] \hat \rho_0) = 0$$ $$\int \text{d}a\,[\hat A_a \hat B_b, \hat B_b \hat A_a] = 0$$
